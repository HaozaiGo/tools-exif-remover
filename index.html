Frequently Asked Questions

What is EXIF data?

EXIF data is metadata embedded in image files, often including camera model, capture date, exposure settings, and GPS coordinates.

Why remove EXIF metadata?

EXIF can reveal private details such as where a photo was taken. Removing it helps protect privacy before sharing images.

Will removing EXIF reduce image quality?

The image may be re-encoded, but the tool uses quality settings intended to preserve visual quality.

Are my photos uploaded?

No. Metadata inspection and cleanup happen in your browser.
